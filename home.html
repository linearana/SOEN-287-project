Welcome to the Campus Resource Booking System

Welcome to the Concordia Resource Booking system, designed to simplify how you reserve campus spaces and equipment. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or staff, this platform helps you book what you need quickly and securely.

What You Can Book

  • Study Rooms: Quiet rooms equipped with Wi-Fi and whiteboards for group or solo study sessions.
  • Engineering Labs: State-of-the-art labs for mechanical, electrical, and computer experiments.
  • Art Studios: Creative spaces for painting, sculpting, and other artistic projects.
  • Film Equipment: Cameras, lighting, and sound kits available for film and media projects.

Who Can Use It?

This system is available to all Concordia students, faculty, and staff. Some resources may require approval or special permissions, which you'll be guided through during booking.

Getting Started

  1. Create an Account: Sign up using your Concordia email to access the booking system.
  2. Browse Resources: Explore available resources and their details on the Resources page.
  3. Make a Booking: Select your desired resource, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking.
  4. Manage Bookings: View, modify, or cancel your bookings through your account dashboard.
  5. Need Help? Contact our support team for assistance with bookings or technical issues.
