Conference Rooms

Conference rooms: Rooms are for meetings, group discussions, and presentations. Not for personal or unrelated activities.
Leave the room clean and tidy after use. Erase whiteboards, remove trash, and reset chairs and tables.
Personal devices may be connected to displays only through approved ports or adapter.
If you no longer need the room, cancel your booking promptly so others can use it.
Arrive on time and do not exceed your reserved time slot.
