Study Rooms

Study rooms are available by reservation only through the online booking system.
Rooms are intended for academic use only such as studying, group projects, tutoring, or meetings related to coursework.
Rooms must be occupied by a minimum of 2 students at all times.
If a group is more than 15 minutes late, the room may be released to other students waiting.
